Four decades, one trajectory.

// SCROLL TO TRAVERSE
1980

GreenField Tractors Co.

Founded by a family of farm‑mechanics in the Midwest, the company built rugged tractors for local growers. The ethos of durability and hands‑on engineering was forged here.

1992

Diversification

Expanded the product line to include combine harvesters and irrigation systems. Modern CNC tooling introduced, laying the groundwork for precise manufacturing practices.

2005

Automotive Components

Entered the automotive supply chain, producing lightweight chassis components for premium manufacturers. First exposure to electric‑driven powertrain parts.

2012

First Electric Prototype

Built an all‑electric utility vehicle for a regional fleet, proving that the company could blend its mechanical heritage with emerging EV tech.

2016

Antigravity Acquisition

Acquired the Antigravity software platform, adding AI‑driven production planning, predictive maintenance, and autonomous driving capabilities to the portfolio.

2020

VIBE Automotives Re‑Brand

The company re‑launched as VIBE Automotives, shifting focus to premium electric vehicles and a fully integrated digital ecosystem.

2022

Launch of VIBE Echelon

Our flagship autonomous luxury sedan debuted, featuring the Antigravity OS, 520 mi EPA range, and a glass‑morphism interior.

2024

Hyper‑Charging Network

Rolled out over 45 k hyper‑charging stations worldwide, delivering up to 350 kW fast‑charge and achieving 99.9 % renewable uptime.

2026 — Today

The Road Ahead

VIBE leads the market with three distinct vehicle families (Echelon, Horizon, Apex), a global supply‑chain hub, and an AI‑first approach to mobility. Our mission: redefine transport, zero emissions, and limitless performance.
