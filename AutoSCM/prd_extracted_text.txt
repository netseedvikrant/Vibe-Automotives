

PRD-03: Supply Chain &amp; Procurement Department Workflow Platform

Product Name: 
AutoSCM
 — Supply Chain &amp; Procurement Workflow SuiteVersion: 1.0

1. Product Overview

AutoSCM
 manages the complete supply chain and procurement lifecycle for an automotive 
organization  from
 supplier identification and onboarding to purchase requisition, RFQ, purchase order, goods receipt, and supplier performance management. It ensures lean procurement with zero parts shortages on the production line.

2. Goals &amp; Objectives

Digitize end-to-end procurement from PR to payment release

Enable supplier onboarding, evaluation, and performance tracking

Provide real-time visibility into PO status, delivery schedules, and inventory

Reduce supplier delivery failures and material shortages

Enable strategic sourcing with cost benchmarking

5. Core Workflows

5.1 Supplier Identification &amp; Onboarding Workflow

Trigger: New part/material sourcing need or existing supplier replacement

Steps:

Sourcing need raised by Engineering/Production (part number, annual volume, technical requirements)

Supplier search: internal database + external search (supplier portal registration)

Potential suppliers invited to submit Expression of Interest (EOI)

EOI documents collected: company profile, financials, quality certifications, capacity

Technical evaluation: Quality team visits (audit) and scores suppliers

Financial evaluation: Finance team reviews supplier financials

Risk assessment: Geographic risk, single-source risk, ESG compliance

Approved suppliers added to Approved Vendor List (AVL)

Onboarding form completed: bank details, GST/VAT, contacts, SLAs

NDA signed electronically (
DocuSign
 integration)

Supplier portal access granted with login credentials

Onboarding notification sent to Procurement, Quality, Finance teams

Fields: Supplier ID, Company Name, Category (Tier-1/Tier-2), Certification (IATF/ISO), Audit Score, Risk Rating, AVL Status, Onboarding Date, Account Manager

5.2 Purchase Requisition (PR) Workflow

Trigger: MRP-generated need or manual request

Steps:

PR auto-created from MRP run or manually raised by Production/Engineering

PR details: part number, description, required quantity, required delivery date, cost 
center

PR routed for approval based on value matrix:

&lt; ₹50,000: Department Head

₹50,000–₹5,00,000: Finance Manager + Department Head

₹5,00,000: CPO + CFO

Approved PR converted to RFQ (if new supplier) or direct PO (if existing contract)

Rejected PR returned with comments for revision

Budget availability check (integration with Finance module)

Fields: PR Number, Date, Requestor, Department, Part Number, Description, Quantity, UOM, Required Date, Cost 
Center
, Budget Code, Estimated Cost, Approval Status, Approver Chain

5.3 RFQ / Tender Workflow

Trigger: Approved PR without existing contract

Steps:

RFQ created from approved PR with technical specifications attached

RFQ sent to 3+ suppliers from AVL (configurable minimum)

Suppliers receive RFQ on portal with deadline for submission

Supplier submits quote: unit price, lead time, payment terms, validity period

Quote comparison matrix auto-generated (price, lead time, quality score)

Commercial negotiation logged in system (bid history tracked)

Negotiated final price documented with justification

Recommended supplier selected with TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) analysis

Sourcing decision approved by CPO

Winning supplier notified; non-winning suppliers receive regret notification

Fields: RFQ ID, PR Reference, Spec Document, Invited Suppliers, Quote Submissions, Lowest Quote, Recommended Supplier, Selection Rationale, TCO Value, Decision Date, Approved By

5.4 Purchase Order (PO) Workflow

Trigger: Sourcing decision approved or contract-based replenishment

Steps:

PO auto-generated from approved sourcing decision or PR

PO details: supplier, line items, quantities, delivery schedule, delivery address, payment terms, tax codes

PO approval routing (value-based matrix same as PR)

Approved PO digitally sent to supplier (email + portal notification)

Supplier acknowledges PO with confirmed delivery date (portal)

PO acknowledgment logged with any deviations flagged

Delivery tracking: supplier updates estimated delivery date on portal

Advance shipping notice (ASN) submitted by supplier before dispatch

GRN process triggered on goods arrival

PO closure after full receipt and invoice matching

Fields: PO Number, PR Reference, Supplier, Line Items, Total Value, Delivery Date, Payment Terms, Tax Amount, Currency, Acknowledgment Status, ASN Number, GRN Status, Invoice Status, PO Status (Open/Partial/Closed)

5.5 Goods Receipt Note (GRN) Workflow

Trigger: Physical goods arrival at plant gate/warehouse

Steps:

Security/Gate entry: vehicle details, supplier, delivery challan number logged

Material tagged to incoming quality inspection (IQC) bay

IQC team performs incoming inspection as per sampling plan (AQL)

IQC result: Accept / Reject / Conditional Accept

Accepted: GRN created in system, material moved to designated warehouse location

Rejected: Rejection Note issued to supplier, return logistics initiated

GRN quantity posted to inventory (ERP integration)

Three-way matching triggered: PO × GRN × Invoice

Matched invoice released to Finance for payment

Unmatched invoice goes to Procurement for resolution

Fields: GRN Number, PO Reference, Supplier, Material, Received Qty, IQC Qty Accepted, Qty Rejected, Rejection Reason, Warehouse Location, Bin Number, Three-Way Match Status, Invoice Number

5.6 Supplier Performance Management Workflow

Trigger: Monthly automated trigger

Steps:

Supplier scorecard auto-generated monthly from system data

Scorecard parameters:

On-time delivery rate (OTD %)

Quality acceptance rate (IQC pass %)

PPAP compliance

Responsiveness (PR-to-PO acknowledgment time)

NCR (Non-Conformance Report) count and closure rate

Scores aggregated into Supplier Rating: Gold / Silver / Bronze / Critical

Critical rating triggers escalation workflow: formal meeting, CAPA required

Supplier reviews CAPA plan on portal, submits within 15 days

Corrective actions tracked to closure

Repeat non-performance → Supplier suspension workflow:

Suspension proposal → CPO approval → Supplier notified → AVL status updated

Annual supplier audit scheduling workflow (linked to Quality module)

Fields: Supplier ID, Evaluation Period, OTD %, IQC Pass %, PPAP Compliance %, NCR Count, Overall Score, Rating, CAPA Required (Y/N), CAPA Status, Audit Due Date

5.7 Shortage Management Workflow

Trigger: MRP shortage alert or production line shortage

Steps:

Shortage alert auto-generated when stock &lt; safety stock level

Production Planner reviews shortage list daily

Expedite PO raised for critical shortage parts (priority flag)

Supplier contacted for acceleration (tracked in system with response logged)

Air freight authorization if critical (requires CPO/CFO approval above threshold)

Alternative sourcing activated (alternate supplier from AVL)

Production schedule adjusted if shortage cannot be met (linked to Manufacturing module)

Root cause documented (supplier failure / forecast error / demand spike)

Safety stock recalculated and updated

Fields: Shortage ID, Part Number, Required Quantity, Available Stock, Gap Quantity, Supplier, PO Date, Expected Delivery, Expedite Status, Air Freight Approved (Y/N), Alternate Source Used, RCA, Safety Stock Updated

5.8 Contract Management Workflow

Trigger: Sourcing decision or contract renewal trigger

Steps:

Contract draft created from standard template (
auto-populated
 from sourcing decision)

Legal review and redlining (with version tracking)

Internal approval: Legal → Finance → CPO

Supplier reviews and signs electronically (
DocuSign
 integration)

Signed contract stored in contract repository

Contract milestone tracker: expiry alerts 90/60/30 days before

Contract renewal workflow triggered at 90-day mark

Price escalation clauses tracked and actioned as per contract terms

Contract performance reviewed quarterly (linked to supplier scorecard)

Fields: Contract ID, Supplier, Contract Type, Start Date, End Date, Total Value, Payment Terms, SLAs, Key Contacts, Renewal Date, Alert Status, Signed Document Link
