Our Circular Blueprint

Traditional automotive manufacturing follows a linear "take-make-waste" philosophy. At VIBE, we are establishing a fully circular supply chain. By linking our live material registries (managed through AutoSCM) with real-time tooling audits (managed in AutoMFG), we monitor the exact carbon cost of every bumper, rotor, and drive unit.

From using bio-composites derived from agricultural waste for interior doors to powering our heavy-duty casting presses with localized geothermal batteries, our production systems operate as ecosystems. We do not just build cleaner cars; we construct cleaner systems.

🔋 Antigravity Battery Recovery

When VIBE battery packs drop below 80% capacity after hundreds of thousands of miles, they are systematically decommissioned and repurposed as grid-stabilization batteries for solar storage before ultimate recycling.

🌊 Ocean Plastic Textiles

VIBE Echelon and Horizon cabins are upholstered with vegan micro-suede and ocean-bound post-consumer plastics, diverting marine waste and eliminating the need for animal products.

⚙️ Smart Press Power Shaving

Our stamping lines monitor live power grids via machine learning algorithms, scheduling high-load press cycles during peak solar abundance hours to reduce grid strain and maximize renewable utility.
