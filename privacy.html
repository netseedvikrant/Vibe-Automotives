PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: June 02, 2026 | Last Updated: June 02, 2026

1. Data Collection & Sources

VIBE Automotives is committed to safeguarding your personal information. We collect data to deliver customized concierge, test drive scheduling, and administrative portal interactions. The specific information we collect includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full Name, Business Email Address, Phone Number, Job Title, and Company Name.
  • Submission Specifics: Vehicle preferences, requested scheduling dates, and uploaded professional materials (such as job application resumes).

We collect this data directly from you via manual input forms on our portal and web presence. Additionally, we may collect technical system metadata through automated cookies or session tracking pixels to optimize portal responsiveness.

2. Purpose of Processing

As an advanced automotive engineering and manufacturing enterprise, we process your personal data under the following legitimate business frameworks:

  • Customer Engagement: Processing inquiries, scheduling corporate concierge services, and managing test drive reservations.
  • Supply Chain Logistics: Managing parts distribution logistics, vehicle assemblies auditing, and vendor communications.
  • Safety & Recalls: Sending required regulatory safety bulletins or vehicle compliance updates associated with vehicle manufacturing and ownership.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or distribute your personal details to outside market brokers. We share data only with verified cloud providers, corporate systems, or legal bodies to fulfill essential manufacturing and portal services:

  • Cloud Hosting Infrastructure: Subprocessors including AWS (Amazon Web Services) and Microsoft Azure for database operations.
  • Customer Relationship Management: Secure cloud-based CRMs including Salesforce and HubSpot.
  • Legal and Regulatory Audits: Verified judicial or governmental institutions where legally mandated to satisfy safety, regulatory, or tax reporting requirements.

4. Data Retention

We operate under strict data minimization guidelines. We retain your contact information and query logs for a period of 5 years after our last documented interaction, unless a longer retention window is mandated by international automotive safety laws, environmental audits, or manufacturing compliance protocols.

5. User Rights ("Right to be Forgotten")

You maintain full control over your personal footprint. Under global privacy protections, you hold the following absolute rights:

  • The Right of Access: You can request a summary or digital copy of all personal information we store about you.
  • The Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete all your collected personal information ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • The Right to Withdraw Consent: You can revoke your consent at any time for active communications or data processing pipelines.

6. Security Measures

VIBE Automotives implements robust, enterprise-grade safety controls to protect database registers against unauthorized penetration or leakage. These include standard Secure Socket Layers (SSL/TLS) traffic encryption, complete database encryption at rest, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and strictly audited role-based access control (RBAC) protocols.

7. Contact and Privacy Requests

For any questions, concerns, or to submit requests for access or erasure under your user rights, please contact our dedicated Data Protection Officer directly at:

Email: privacy@vibeauto.com
