About VIBE

Pioneering the future of mobility through AI‑driven intelligence.

VIBE Automotives fuses cutting‑edge Smart OS, next‑gen electric powertrains, and a global hyper‑charging network to deliver a seamless, zero‑emission experience for enterprise fleets, premium consumers, and high‑performance enthusiasts.

  • 🔧 Integrated SCM & R&D: Real‑time supply‑chain visibility across all three VIBE models.
  • ⚡ Hyper‑Charging: 45 k+ stations worldwide, 350 kW DC fast charge, 99.9 % renewable uptime.
  • 🧭 Autonomous Driving: AI Autopilot v4.2 (Level 4) with predictive routing.
  • 🌱 Sustainability: 100 % carbon‑neutral production, recycled‑material interiors, and closed‑loop battery recycling.

Since our launch in 2022, VIBE has empowered over 200 k vehicles on the road, partnered with Fortune 500 enterprises, and earned multiple Innovation Awards for safety, design, and environmental stewardship.

VIBE Hypercharging Network

Global Supercharger Infrastructure

VIBE's global charging infrastructure represents the world's most advanced, ultra-high-speed network. Spanning over 45,000 Hypercharging points across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, our stations deliver up to 350 kW DC fast charging, capable of charging a VIBE vehicle from 10% to 80% in under 15 minutes.

Designed with micro-grid solar integration and battery buffering systems, our charging hubs boast 99.9% uptime and are powered by 100% renewable energy, delivering a seamless, carbon-neutral charging experience globally.
